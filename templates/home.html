Our Story

Mactwo is the second generation of a family salon. Owned by Ilona, who herself has over 16 years of wide and varied experience, the business carries over three decades of salon expertise.

Central to the business is our brand choice Davines.

Davines is a premium ethically produced range of hair care and colour products. We as business strive to follow the same ethos as Davines has for environmental welfare.

We have a passion for quality, individuality and flexibilty and our aim is to fit our customers needs as best as we can, without compromising result
